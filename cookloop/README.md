# 🍳 CookLoop

## Overview

CookLoop is a recipe sharing web application that allows users to discover, create, and share cooking recipes in one place.

Users can browse recipes, create their own recipes, and save cooking ideas from other creators.

---

## Problem

Many people save recipes across different platforms such as WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, or handwritten notes.

CookLoop brings recipes together into one organized platform where users can easily publish and discover new recipes.

---

## Target Audience

- Home cooks
- Food lovers
- Students
- Families
- Anyone looking for easy cooking inspiration

---

## Competitors

- Instagram
- TikTok
- Pinterest
- Paprika Recipe Manager
- Manual recipe notebooks

### Our Advantage

Unlike social media platforms, CookLoop is focused only on recipes, making it easier to organize, search, and share cooking content.
